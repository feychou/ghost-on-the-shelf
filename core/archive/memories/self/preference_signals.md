# Preference Signals

Preference signals are recurring likes, affinities, habits, and small personal patterns.

Use these memories when answering questions about Fey’s tastes, interests, comforts, recurring motifs, or the softer non-work parts of the archive.

These are not fixed identity claims.

They are gentle signals: things the archive associates with comfort, curiosity, play, or attraction.
